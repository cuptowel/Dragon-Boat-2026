On the Water

Race Rules for ET Dragon

👥
Paddler Counts
Min 12 · Max 18 paddlers per boat (+drummer +steerer, no gender restrictions on those). Mixed division: max 10 male, min 4 female paddlers. In adverse weather, organiser may reduce per-boat count.
⏱
Pre-Race Sign-In
Report to sign-in counter 3 races before your race. Tell officials your race number. Show Team Card. All paddlers must be at the boat marshalling area (lanes 1–13).
🚣
Boat Assignment
Odd races (R1,3,5…) = Yellow Plate boats. Even races (R2,4,6…) = White Plate boats. Number plates on pontoon are always on your right side.
🥁
Start Procedure
Drummer holds bow line & sits facing forward (sitting rule applies). Paddles may stay in water at start. Steerer aligns white dot on stern to 1st handrail. Only release handle after starting horn. Pushing/pulling handle = FOUL.
🚦
Start Signal & False Starts
Signal: "Are you ready?" → "Ready" → Horn / GO. Two-start rule: only 1st false start recalled. 2nd start always goes — any team that jumps is disqualified at finish.
🏁
Course & Finish
270m total. No re-race after first 50m. Stay in your lane — numbered buoys always on right. Timing taken from tip of dragon boat crossing virtual finish line (~1–5m before final buoys). Slow down after finish to avoid accidents.
🧰
Equipment
All equipment provided by organiser. You may bring: own drumsticks, bailers, sponge. Whistles forbidden. Only organiser's paddles allowed — spare paddles must stay in boat. Protect all equipment.
📋
Protests & Appeals
Protest: within 15 min, HK$500, white form. Appeal: 15 min, HK$1,000, yellow form. Video review: 15 min, HK$2,000. All must be signed by Team Manager + show Team Card. Fee refunded if successful.
Scoring System
Each round is scored. 1st place earns an extra point. Final ranking = sum of points across all rounds. Ties broken by final round result. Divisions going direct to finals (scored cumulatively): Mixed Open (excl. Heats) · Men's Plate · Ladies · Stanley MedMA Cup. Divisions using flow chart: Mixed Open (Heats only) · Men's Cup · Shangri-La Mixed Corporate.

Race Day

Pre-Race Sign-In Process

01
Report to Sign-In Counter
Go to the sign-in counter (next to information counter & prize stage) 3 races before your assigned race.
02
State Race Number
Tell officials your race number. Present your Team Card. This is mandatory — no card, no start.
03
All Members to Marshalling Area
All paddlers stand by at the boat marshalling area. Follow officials' instructions when boarding. U-turn only after clearing the shallow area (buoy-marked).
If Applicable

Junk Usage Rules

⚓
Collect by 16 June
All junk flags, speedboat flags, and shuttle boat tickets must be collected from the Training Centre by 16 June 2026.
📏
Large Junks
Junks over 70 ft should moor further from shore at deeper water positions.
🧮
Head Count
Conduct a head count before and after boarding the junk. No exceptions.
🚫
No Water Sports · No Alcohol During Race
All water sports prohibited — DO NOT SWIM. Do not drink during racing. Save it for after! Drink/drug boating is a criminal offence (HK$25,000 fine + 3 years jail).
